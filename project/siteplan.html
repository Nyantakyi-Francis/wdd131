StudyHub – Site Plan

Site Name

StudyHub – A Smart Study Companion for Students

This name reflects the core purpose of the site: to serve as a centralized hub where students can access study resources, tools, and a supportive learning community.

Optional domain: studyhub-gh.org

Site Purpose

StudyHub is a responsive educational web platform that helps students excel academically. It provides: concise subject summaries, a searchable archive of past questions, a study planner tool, downloadable study resources, and—optionally—a discussion forum. Its goal is to empower students, especially in Ghana, to prepare better for exams and cultivate independent study habits.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find past BECE and WASSCE questions with explanations?
  • How can I build a daily or weekly study timetable?
  • What tips or resources can help me prepare for my final exams more effectively?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color – #0a66c2 (Study Blue): Used for headings, buttons, and main interface highlights.
  • Accent Color – #f39c12 (Golden Orange): Used for icons, link hovers, and call-to-action areas.
  • Background – #f9fafa: Used as the base background for clarity and lightness.

Typography

  • Primary Font – Segoe UI: Used for body text and navigation items for clarity and readability.
  • Headings – Segoe UI Bold: Used for major section titles and banner headlines for emphasis.
